DRINK INC - RELAX AT THE DRINK BAR

Whether your just about to work out or finished a session, everyones welcome in our Relaxation Zone.

Drink inc opened its doors to a brand new health bar at Zone Fitness offering nutritious protein drinks, shakes and tasty health snacks.

Their super slick branding & ethos is right on par with Zone Fitness core values. It is very important that the gym members are able to access good quality food, drinks and protein shakes pre & post workout.

We are able to work individually with members to ensure that they get the nutrients that they require. At Drink inc we are looking forward to offering healthy breakfast & lunch options to the Granby Industrial Estate and Link Park workers.

 




Sit back & relax, meet up with friends pre or post workout. Use our App to listen to our big screen TV. We will also offer a pre-order service to members who are on a tight timescale. Order pre-workout & pick up post-workout. Perfect for if you need to get back to work quick!

 








Adding more of a social aspect to the gym enables it to be a place where people can meet, chat and have some down time with friends, it should not be just a place where you will workout & leave. Drink inc can not only be used by Zone members, but also by the public & local businesses too.

For those who dont want to stay, we still offer a selection of health drinks & snacks from our vending machines. You will also get chance to purchase our branded gym towels & bottles. Padlocks for the lockers are also available from our vending machines too.
