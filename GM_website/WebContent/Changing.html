LUXURY CHANGING FACILITIES

CHANGING ROOMS

Our luxury changing facilities have individual cubicle showers, toilets and locker facilities. There are hairdryers available too. Just remember to bring a towel, your toiletries and a padlock with you if you wish to use our facilities when you visit our gym. But don’t worry if you forget, we have branded towels and padlocks are available from vending machines near reception.

We also offer disabled shower and toilet facilities.


 





We have large, luxury changing rooms with lockers to keep yourpersonal belongings safe. Padlocks are available at the vending machines in the Relaxation Zone.


 







Our fully air conditioned changing areas and washing facilities are clean and spacious areas, with large mirrors and hair dryer facilities to provide you great quality and comfort.


 








We have cubicle showering facilities providing hair and body wash, state of the art shower systems, providing all the privacy you need.


 












We offer alarmed disabled shower and toilet facilities, with disable lift for ease of movement around the building.
