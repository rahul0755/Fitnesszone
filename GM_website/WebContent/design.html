THE DESIGN

Easily located just of the Jurassic cycleway on the Granby Industrial Estate, with ample parking for 150 vehicles & a secure, lockable, covered bike shelter.

Our urban industrial construction, mixing raw exposed beams, raw materials & sleek simplicity is ultimately designed with fitness in mind.

At 19,000 square feet in total Zone fitness boasts over 300 pieces of superior gym equipment! These include 15 treadmills & 9 cross trainers, so no more waiting around for something to become free ! We have set our standard high & have included quality equipment from True, Exigo & Stages.

A vast amount of time & money has been spent in designing & making sure your experience at the gym is spent in near perfect conditions, with our state of the art air management system which can be viewed for all to see, as part of our industrial chic image. It will keep you comfortable whatever time of year it is!
